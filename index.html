Gwaa

an early (not-very-polished) prototype of an entry for the 2022 7-Day Roguelike Challenge

the idea is a magic-themed deckbuilding game where your mana is represented by a hand of cards with the various types of mana (and you can upgrade your deck by getting cards that give more mana, and get better spells, and so on)

use WASD or HJKL or arrow keys to move; P/N or [/] to change targets, > to go down stairs, and the number keys to cast spells (healing will heal yourself; attack spells will target whichever enemy is highlighted)
